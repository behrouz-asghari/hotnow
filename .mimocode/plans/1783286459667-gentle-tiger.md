### 2. Use `persian-swear-words` + custom sexual phrase filter

**File:** `app/components/TrendsLabelsBoard.tsx`

**Problem:** `persian-swear-words.hasSwear()` splits text by spaces and checks each word individually. It catches single swear words (e.g. "سکس" in a film title) but CANNOT catch:
- Multi-word sexual phrases: "پوزیشن‌های جنسی", "آمیزش جنسی", "روش‌های آمیزش"
- Implicit sexual terms: "زن در بالا" (sexual position), "مرد در پایین"
- Contextual sexual content that uses non-swear words

**Solution:** Combine both approaches:
1. Install `persian-swear-words` — for comprehensive single-word swear filtering
2. Keep a custom `SEXUAL_PHRASES` array for multi-word sexual phrases and implicit terms
3. `isSexualContent()` checks BOTH:
   - `PersianSwear.hasSwear(text)` for single swear words
   - Regex match against `SEXUAL_PHRASES` for multi-word/implicit terms

**Custom phrase list categories:**
- Sexual positions: "زن در بالا", "مرد در پایین", "پوزیشن زن بالا", etc.
- Sexual acts: "پوزیشن‌های جنسی", "آمیزش جنسی", "روش‌های آمیزش", "کنش جنسی"
- Sexual content: "فیلم سکسی", "صحنه سکسی", "ویدیو سکسی"
- Body parts (contextual): "سینه", "باسن" (when in sexual context)
